Animes

Death Note

Supernatural, Suspense

2006

Brutal murders, petty thefts, and senseless violence pollute the human world. The ingenious 17-year-old student Light Yagami and the god of death Ryuk share one belief: their worlds are rotten. Together, they set out to change the world with the power of the Death Note.

Psycho Pass

Action, Mystery

2012

In the 22nd century, Japan enforces the Sibyl System, an objective way of measuring each citizen's mental state for criminal intent. Those deemed dangerous are apprehended or eliminated. Justice, and how it is enforced, has forever changed.

That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime

Action, Adventure

2018

Satoru Mikami is a typical corporate worker whose life is cut short when he is stabbed on the street. As he succumbs to his injuries, a voice recites commands that he cannot understand. Reincarnated as a slime in a new world, Satoru gains powers and begins a new adventure.

Black Clover

Action, Fantasy

2017

Asta and Yuno were abandoned at the same church and grew up competing to become the next Wizard King. Yuno wields incredible magic, while Asta has none, relying on physical training. Despite their differences, they are determined to fulfill their dream.

Death Parade

Drama, Supernatural

2017

After death, humans are judged at Quindecim, a bar where Decim, the arbiter, passes judgment through life-threatening games. These games determine whether the soul will reincarnate or be sent into the void. The stakes are life and death, and Decim holds the final say.

Demon Slayer

Action, Fantasy

2019

Tanjirou Kamado supports his family after the death of his father. One day, after a trip to the village, Tanjirou discovers that his family has been slaughtered by demons. He sets out to avenge them and protect his sister, the only survivor, who has been turned into a demon.

Golden Kamuy

Action, Adventure

2018

Saichi Sugimoto, an ex-soldier from the Russo-Japanese war, seeks gold in Hokkaido to fulfill a promise to his fallen friend. He learns of a fortune in stolen Ainu gold, hidden and mapped on the bodies of convicts. Sugimoto embarks on a dangerous treasure hunt.

Hunter x Hunter

Action, Adventure

2011

Hunters accomplish dangerous tasks, from exploring uncharted territories to capturing rare creatures. To become a Hunter, candidates must pass the grueling Hunter Examination. Gon, the protagonist, embarks on this journey to find his long-lost father.

Monster

Drama, Mystery

2004

Dr. Kenzou Tenma, a skilled neurosurgeon, faces a moral dilemma when ordered to prioritize a famous patient over an immigrant worker. This decision changes his life when the boy he saves grows up to become a monstrous killer. Dr. Tenma now seeks redemption by hunting down the monster he saved.

Kaiji

Suspense

2007

Kaiji Itou is a down-and-out gambler pressured by loan sharks to settle a large debt. Given a chance to erase his debts, Kaiji boards the Espoir, where one night of gambling could change his life. But the stakes are deadly, and survival is not guaranteed.

My Hero Academia

Action

2016

In a world where 80% of humanity has superpowers called "quirks," Izuku Midoriya is born powerless. Despite this, he dreams of becoming a hero. His life changes when he meets All Might, the world's greatest hero, who grants him the power to fulfill his dream.

Parasyte: The Maxim

Action, Horror

2014

Parasitic aliens descend upon Earth, taking control of human brains and morphing their bodies to feed on others. Shinichi Izumi is infected, but the parasite in his body fails to take over his brain. Together, they must coexist and battle other parasites threatening humanity.
